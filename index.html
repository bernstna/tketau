Who We Are

The Tau Chapter

Founded in 1914, the Tau Chapter of Tau Kappa Epsilon has been shaping the lives of men for over a century. Success doesn't just come from a degree, it comes from the growth and connections made throughout your experience.

Whether you want to give back to St. Jude, hit the books during Sunday study sessions, join the executive leadership board, travel to national conferences, compete on the intramural field, or make lifelong memories at our brotherhood/function nights, we truly have something for everyone.

If you're looking for brothers who'll push to be a better man academically, socially, and physically, you're in the right place.

— The Brothers of Tau Chapter

Philanthropy - St. Jude

Founded by a TKE

Every year, Tau Chapter raises funds and awareness for St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, because families dealing with childhood cancer should never have to worry about a bill.

From our annual campus gala to the St. Jude Campus in Memphis, TN, to smaller fundraisers throughout the year, philanthropy is inseperable from our mission to create better men for a better world.
